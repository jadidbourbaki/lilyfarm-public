Vision

Lily Farm is named after the developer's family cat Lily, whose picture is included below. We want to create Free and Open-Source platforms that help make the lives of working class people better. The plan is for Lily Farm to eventually become a global resource for people looking for healthy food grown in their community. We also intend to include information on which Farmers' Markets support social subsidies such as the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) and the Farmers' Market Nutrition Program (FMNP) in the United States.

Questions and Feedback
